Accordion

Interior will work at any location that uses Dropbox. Note that if Bluetooth is disabled on a company’s iPad, you’ll be unable to use Interior while the “Dropbox Interior is offline” notice is on the iPad screen.

Anyone with an iPhone or Android phone can use Interior.

If you have Dropbox at your office, you can use the Interior app to set your personal notification preferences. Also note: your Interior will not appear on your company’s iPad, but it will work when you visit other locations with Dropbox.

Dropbox iPads recognize Interior using Apple’s iBeacon technology. Since Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) is the underlying communication method for iBeacons, you’ll need to enable Bluetooth on your iPhone or Android phone.

Without Bluetooth, the Beacons will not “see” you, so you will not be able to use Interior.

This is a common misconception, but today’s Bluetooth technology, known as Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE), is different than the outdated Classic Bluetooth. BLE draws very little power and will have minimal impact on your phone’s battery life.
